About ASITFP

The Ashoka Society for International Trade and Foreign Policy (ASITFP) held its inaugural event on [Event Date] at [Event Venue]. The event was a significant milestone in launching ASITFP's activities and providing a platform for students interested in international relations and diplomacy.

Ambassador Mohan Kumar, a distinguished diplomat with 36 years of service in the Indian Foreign Services, graced the event as the Guest of Honor. His illustrious career includes notable roles such as India's lead negotiator at the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT) and the World Trade Organization (WTO). He is also the Chairman of the Research and Information System for Developing Countries (RIS) and has served as Joint Secretary at the Ministry of External Affairs.

The event commenced with the formal introduction of ASITFP, followed by an enlightening training bootcamp led by Ambassador Kumar. This training aimed to equip participants with essential skills in the fields of foreign policy and diplomacy. The bootcamp was a valuable opportunity for attendees to learn from Ambassador Kumar's extensive expertise.

A luncheon, thoughtfully organized by ASITFP, facilitated informal interactions between Ambassador Kumar and the attendees. This allowed participants to gain personal insights and engage in discussions about various aspects of international relations.

The inaugural event held special significance as an integral part of ASITFP's induction program. The training bootcamp, led by Ambassador Kumar, offered participants a unique chance to learn practical skills and gain insights into the intricate world of diplomacy. It was particularly beneficial for those interested in International Relations and International Economics.

To ensure the event's success and relevance, ASITFP conducted a survey to gauge student interest prior to extending an official invitation to Ambassador Kumar. The valuable feedback received will inform future engagements and events. Attendees were also informed about upcoming inductions, which will take place following this event and shortly before the next.

The inaugural event of ASITFP marked the beginning of an exciting journey towards fostering a deeper understanding of international trade and foreign policy. Ambassador Mohan Kumar's presence and the active engagement of participants highlighted the enthusiasm for learning and exploring the realms of diplomacy and global relations.
